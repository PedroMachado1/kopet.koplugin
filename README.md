# KoPet - Virtual Pet for KOReader

A feature-rich virtual pet plugin for KOReader that turns your reading habit into an immersive RPG experience. Level up your companion, track your reading stats, and discover unique evolution paths based on your habits.

## ✨ Features

### 🎮 Game Mechanics
- **Dynamic Stats**: Manage your pet's Hunger, Happiness, and Energy.
- **Leveling System**: Earn XP for every page you read. Watch your pet grow from an Egg to a Legendary entity.
- **Reading Streaks**: Keep your daily reading habit alive to earn XP multipliers.
- **Inventory System**: 
    - **Food**: Found randomly while reading to keep hunger at bay.
    - **Treats**: Earned through book progress milestones.
    - **Crystals**: Rewarded for finishing books to boost development.
    - **Medicine**: Required to cure your pet if it falls sick from neglect.

### 🦊 Intelligent Evolutions
Your pet doesn't just grow; it adapts to **you**. At level 6, your reading habits determine its path:
- **Night Owl**: Emerges if you predominantly read during late hours.
- **Scholar**: Forms if you prefer long, focused reading sessions at a steady pace.
- **Speedster Fox**: Evolves if you are a fast-paced reader who devours pages.

### 🎩 Accessories & Journal
- **Rare Drops**: Discover equippable accessories like Hats, Glasses, and Wands as you read.
- **Pet Journal**: A persistent log of your journey together, marking level-ups, evolutions, and major milestones.

## ⚙️ Customization Menu
A dedicated "KoPet" menu is added to your KOReader Tools, allowing you to:
- **Difficulty Modes**: Choose between Easy, Normal, and Hard to balance food drops vs. nutrition.
- **Rename Pet**: Give your companion a unique name.
- **View Stats**: Detailed breakdown of level progress, reading speed, and lifetime statistics.
- **Language**: Full support for Portuguese and English (automatically detects KOReader settings).

## 🚀 How to Install
1. Download the `kopet.koplugin` folder.
2. Navigate to the KOReader directory on your device: `koreader/plugins/`.
3. Copy the `kopet.koplugin` folder into the `plugins/` directory.
4. Restart KOReader.
5. Open a book, tap the top menu -> **Tools (Gear/Screwdriver icon)** -> **KoPet**.

## 🛠️ Usage
- **View Pet**: Opens the main interface to see your pet's ASCII art and status bars.
- **Feed/Pet**: Interact with your companion to maintain its stats.
- **Accessories**: Equip found items to customize your pet's look.
- **Journal**: Review your reading history and pet milestones.

Enjoy your new reading companion!
